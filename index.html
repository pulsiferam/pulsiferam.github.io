Announcing: Changeling

Tek & Code Studios has begun development of our new game: Changeling. Imagine waking up one day and realizing that for your whole life, you've been living with the wrong family, in the wrong town, in the wrong world. All around Kyril, an unusual boy with an almost fey-like appearance, mysterious things begin to occur. Kyril soon realizes that there is a great danger coming, and he cannot stop it alone.
