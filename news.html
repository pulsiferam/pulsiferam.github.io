Announcing: Changeling

May 24, 2017

Tek & Code Studios has begun development of our new game: Changeling. In a world of engineering and steam-powered machines, primal wild magic is afoot. All around Kyril, an unusual boy with an almost fey-like appearance, mysterious things begin to occur. Kyril soon realizes that there is a great danger coming, and he cannot stop it alone.

Keep an eye out for more updates!
